PEIYAO, QU

I am a research student at the Graduate School of Informatics of Kyoto University. My current research interests include domain-specific accelerators and SFQ superconducting microprocessor.

I received M.E in Institute of Computing Technology, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2020. I graduated from Wuhan Institute of Technology with a B.E in Software Engineering in 2017.
